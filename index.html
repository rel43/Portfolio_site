My name is Savva, I am 19 years old and I live in Saint Petersburg. I am currently studying Garment Construction as my second education in the sewing field.

I love drawing and creating patterns, and I feel confident in my skills. In my free time, I really enjoy creating illustrations, working on draping on mannequins, and sewing for myself.

You can see some of my personal artwork in the Interests section. I enjoy learning and getting inspired by different ideas.

My goals:

  • Learn 3D design and start working in Blender and CLO
  • Improve skills in graphic design software
  • Enhance sewing and modeling abilities for complex garments
  • Improve my English to a professional level
